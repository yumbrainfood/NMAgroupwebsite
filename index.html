About the Exhibition

The exhibition explores themes of self, altered realities, and the complicated and fragile experience of personal discovery. Through website design, videography, photography, and multimedia installations, they offer a captivating journey of soul searching, inviting viewers to join them in questioning their own perceptions.

"At the heart of our exhibition, there is a deep exploration of the subconscious," explains Keith, one of the participants.

The exhibition serves as an open dialogue, encouraging viewers to challenge their perspectives and engage in thought-provoking introspection.

The "OMNILOGUE" exhibition will be open to the public on May 20th, from 3pm-5pm. Admission is free, and all are welcome to experience this immersive exploration of creativity and self-discovery.
