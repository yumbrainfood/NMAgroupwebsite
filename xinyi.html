Rainy Day

Xinyi Ju

View project ↓

Artist Statement

This is the story of a girl who was sexually harassed on a rainy day. The girl encounters sexual harassment on a bus on a rainy day, and the indifference of those around her sends the clueless girl into a state of fear and disbelief; she begins to question whether it was her own fault that led to the sexual harassment, and transfers her fear to the rainy day, and henceforth fears the rainy day and the sound of raindrops. On a rainy day, the girl recalls the experience of sexual harassment at that time and gradually self-eyes abhorrence and self-doubt, gradually unable to get out of this negative emotion.
