Brain Food

Dorwin Liang

View project ↓

Artist Statement

This piece, titled “Brain Food” focuses on the mind’s ability to shape your reality through the thoughts, feelings, and emotions that you consciously or subconsciously feed your brain. The mind possesses the power to manifest thoughts and emotions into reality and alter the way you perceive the world around you. In this web based piece, you play the role of a student going through a day in the life with various scenarios where the thoughts and emotions you feed your brain can be physically seen and shown on the screen. You’ll be able to understand the mind’s powerful ability to shape your perception of the world as a result of feeding your mind with either positive or negative emotions.
